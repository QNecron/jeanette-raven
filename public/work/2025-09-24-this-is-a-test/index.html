Team + Role

Team:
  • 2 designers, Strategist, Content + Conversation Designer
  • Product, AI/ML Engineering partners
  • UX Program Manager
My Role:
  • Defined AI experience strategy, vision, and roadmap

  • Presented vision to executives to secure buy-in and alignment
